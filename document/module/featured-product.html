Boss - Featured Product

Boss - Feature Product module allows you to display products according to popular, best seller, special, latest products or choose product by name.

In back end, go to Admin CP / Extensions / Modules / Boss - Feature Product > Edit

  • 1. Module Name: Write a name for this module
  • 2. Status: You can disable or enable this extension.
  • 3. Get product from: The products you want to show.
  • 4. Image (W x H): The width and height of product images.
  • 5. Description length: The maximum character length of description.
  • 6. Limit: The maximum number of products will be displayed.
  • 7. Column: The column of products will be displayed.
  • 8. Product Layout: Show products by list or grid.
  • 9. Style:The layout style will be appeared in the front-end.
  • 10. Title: This is the title of this module that will appear in the front end. You can leave it empty if you don't need a title.
  • 11. Description: This is a description that will appear below the title. You can leave it empty if you don't need a description.
  • 12. Left Banner: The banner appear in the left of module. Leave this banner empty to disable it.
  • 13. Right Banner: The banner appear in the right of module. Leave this banner empty to disable it.
  • 14. Use Scrolling Panel: Products slide function.
  • 15. Product Large: Show the larger product you want your customers to pay attention to it.
